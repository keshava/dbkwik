Hello,
thank you for participating in our survey.
It is about finding a corresponding wiki page of one wiki in another similar wiki.
You can use the search functionality of the wiki itself, using google or any other search strategy.
Please note that the page can be named in a different way (USA can be also matched to United States or America).
Use a recent version of Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer or Opera for this survey.
