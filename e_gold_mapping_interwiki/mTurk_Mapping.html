Hello,
thank you for participating in our survey.
It is about finding a corresponding wiki page of one wiki in two different but similar wikis.
You can use the search functionality of the wiki itself, using google or any other search strategy.
Please note that the page can be named in a different way (USA can be also matched to United States or America).
Use a recent version of Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer or Opera for this survey and have javascript enabled.
You have 40 minutes at maximum for this survey which means for each wikipage 2 minutes.
